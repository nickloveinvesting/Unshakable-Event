### Core Frameworks Taught

**The Daily Integrity Protocol (Three Pillars)**

**Pillar 1: Mental Sharpening**
- Components: Morning focus journal (3 priorities, 1 distraction guard), midday mental check-in, evening reflection (3 wins, 1 lesson, 1 gratitude)
- Application method: 5-10 minute micro-journaling with uncomfortable self-reflection prompts
- Common mistakes: Surface-level answers, avoiding real introspection
- Success indicators: Increased mental clarity, decision-making speed, self-awareness

**Pillar 2: Spiritual Centering**
- Components: Morning scripture meditation (10 min minimum), midday prayer pause, evening gratitude surrender
- Application method: Radical honesty in prayer/reflection, anchoring in foundational truths
- Common mistakes: Going through religious motions vs. seeking transformation
- Success indicators: Internal peace, purpose clarity, reduced anxiety

**Pillar 3: Physical Activation and Recovery**
- Components: Morning movement (stretching/bodyweight exercises), midday mobility, evening restoration
- Application method: Intentional discomfort introduction to break comfort dependency
- Common mistakes: Making excuses based on equipment, weather, or time
- Success indicators: Increased energy, mental clarity, proof of self-command

**The Inversion Framework**
- Step 1: Identify current habit loops and self-limiting stories
- Step 2: Challenge cherished assumptions ("I'm not a morning person")
- Step 3: Interrupt patterns with deliberate counter-actions
- Step 4: Replace eliminated behaviors with integrity-aligned alternatives
- Success metric: Moving from addition-focused to subtraction-focused improvement
