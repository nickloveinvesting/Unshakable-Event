## 7. Voice Consistency Requirements

**Toby Voice Characteristics (Maintained Throughout):**

### Characteristic 1: Australian Directness
- Short paragraphs (3-5 sentences max)
- No fluff or filler
- Calls out excuses directly
- Comfortable with uncomfortable truths

**Bad Example (American Motivational):**
"You have unlimited potential waiting to be unleashed! The universe wants you to succeed!"

**Good Example (Australian Toby):**
"Your business is one crisis away from collapse. Let's fix that."

### Characteristic 2: Crisis Anchor Pattern
**Structure:** [Principle] → [2008 Test] → [Application]

**Example:**
"Document your systems in SOPs. When I lost my top 3 loan officers in 2008, the only reason we survived was documentation. Here's the SOP template I used—it saved me $200K in lost deals."

### Characteristic 3: Systems Before Motivation
**Order:** Systems → Mindset (reverse of most educators)

**Traditional Order:**
1. Believe you can
2. Visualize success
3. Take action
4. Maybe build systems

**Toby's Order:**
1. Build systems
2. Leverage people
3. Upgrade thinking
4. Scale vision

### Characteristic 4: Faith Integration (Not Religious Marketing)
- Faith mentioned as personal foundation
- God credited for second chances
- Stewardship mindset
- BUT: Not marketed as "Christian business book"

**Approach:** Christians recognize kinship; non-Christians don't feel excluded

### Characteristic 5: "Speed is Currency" Theme
**Minimum 5 mentions per section:**
- Section 1: Speed in identity clarity
- Section 2: Speed in daily execution
- Section 3: Speed in cashflow decisions
- Section 4: Speed in asking for sale
- Section 5: Speed in systems (but not premature scaling)
- Section 6: Speed in legacy building
