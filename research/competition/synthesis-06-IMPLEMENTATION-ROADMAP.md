### Phase 4: Layout & Design (Weeks 25-30)
**Duration:** 6 weeks
**Deliverable:** Print-ready PDF (200-250 pages)

**Design Specifications:**
- Page size: 8.5" x 11" (letter size, workbook format)
- Binding: Spiral or perfect-bound (user preference)
- Paper: Heavy weight (80+ lb) for durability
- Color: Full-color interior (visuals, diagrams, photos)
- Cover: Hard cover or thick card stock

**Layout Requirements:**
- Wide margins for note-taking
- Exercise pages with fill-in-the-blank space
- Worksheet pages perforated or copyable
- Section dividers clearly marked
- Page numbers, headers, footers consistent

**Success Metric:** Professional appearance matching or exceeding competitors (Ziglar Performance Planner quality standard).
