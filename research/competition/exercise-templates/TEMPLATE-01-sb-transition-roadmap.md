## Exercise Purpose

This exercise helps you map your current position in Kiyosaki's Cashflow Quadrant and create a concrete plan to transition from Self-Employed (S) to Business Owner (B). Toby made this exact transition from solo mortgage broker to 35+ loan officer empire—twice.

**The Four Quadrants:**
- **E (Employee):** You trade time for money working for someone else
- **S (Self-Employed):** You own your job; business depends on you
- **B (Business Owner):** You own systems; business runs without you
- **I (Investor):** Your money works for you

**Toby's Journey:**
- 1998: S Quadrant (solo broker with $600)
- 2003: B Quadrant (35+ officers, systems-driven)
- 2008: Crisis revealed system weaknesses
- 2012: Rebuilt stronger B Quadrant (anti-fragile systems)

## Part 1: Current State Assessment

### Your Current Quadrant

**Instructions:** Answer honestly. No one sees this but you.

**Question 1:** What happens to your income if you take a 2-week vacation?

- [ ] **Nothing—income continues** → You're in B or I Quadrant
- [ ] **Income drops significantly** → You're in S Quadrant
- [ ] **Income stops completely** → You're in E or early S Quadrant

**Question 2:** What percentage of your business revenue depends on your personal delivery?

- [ ] **0-20%** → Strong B Quadrant
- [ ] **21-50%** → Transitioning S→B
- [ ] **51-80%** → Solidly S Quadrant
- [ ] **81-100%** → Early S or high-level E

**Question 3:** If you disappeared for 90 days, what would happen to your business?

- [ ] **It would run smoothly** → B Quadrant (systems)
- [ ] **It would struggle but survive** → Transitioning S→B
- [ ] **It would fail within 30 days** → S Quadrant (you ARE the business)
- [ ] **It would fail immediately** → Early S or E

**Question 4:** How many people could replicate your core work if given your systems/documentation?

- [ ] **5+ people could do it** → Strong systems (B)
- [ ] **2-4 people could do it** → Decent systems (late S/early B)
- [ ] **1 person could do it** → Weak systems (S)
- [ ] **No one could do it** → Tribal knowledge (pure S)

**Your Current Quadrant (circle one):**

**E** | **S** | **Transitioning S→B** | **B** | **I**
