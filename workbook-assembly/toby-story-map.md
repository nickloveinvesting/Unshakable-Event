## Primary Story Assets

### Story Asset 1: Farm Foundations
**Core Narrative:** Growing up on the farm, early work ethic formation
**Emotional Core:** Humble beginnings, discipline born of necessity, character before success
**Key Details:**
- Early morning chores before school
- Learning the value of consistent daily effort
- Father's model of quiet discipline
- Connection between physical labor and mental strength
- No shortcuts on a farm - everything earned

### Story Asset 2: The $600 Beginning
**Core Narrative:** Starting Imperial Mortgage with $600 and a dream
**Emotional Core:** Courage, faith, starting before you're ready
**Key Details:**
- The specific moment of decision
- What that $600 represented (everything he had)
- First office, first client, first deal
- The fear and the faith in equal measure
- "Speed is Currency" birth - couldn't afford to wait

### Story Asset 3: Building Imperial Mortgage
**Core Narrative:** Growing from solo operator to successful mortgage company
**Emotional Core:** Systems, sales mastery, team building
**Key Details:**
- First hire, first systems
- Learning sales through trial and error
- Developing scripts that worked
- Building a culture of excellence
- The pride of creating something real

### Story Asset 4: The 2008 Collapse
**Core Narrative:** Financial crisis devastation - losing nearly everything
**Emotional Core:** Devastation, questioning, dark night of the soul, faith tested
**Key Details:**
- The phone calls with bad news
- Watching the business crumble
- Financial devastation specifics
- Impact on family (Taylor and Amber watching)
- The moment of deepest despair
- What almost broke him

### Story Asset 5: The Pivot & Survival
**Core Narrative:** Immediate crisis response and survival mode
**Emotional Core:** Resilience, adaptability, doing whatever it takes
**Key Details:**
- First 24-48 hours after realization
- Triage decisions made
- What got cut, what got kept
- Communication with family, team, creditors
- The daily discipline that kept him going

### Story Asset 6: The Faith Anchor
**Core Narrative:** Spiritual foundation that held during crisis
**Emotional Core:** Faith transcending circumstances, purpose beyond profit
**Key Details:**
- Specific prayers, scriptures, moments
- Church community support
- How faith changed his perspective
- "God's not done with me yet" moment
- Faith as identity anchor when business identity shattered

### Story Asset 7: The Comeback - Global Integrity Finance
**Core Narrative:** Rising from ashes to build something greater
**Emotional Core:** Redemption, wisdom gained, building differently
**Key Details:**
- The decision to rebuild, not retreat
- Why "Global Integrity" - naming the values
- What he did differently this time
- Systems and safeguards built from lessons learned
- Building with legacy in mind from day one

### Story Asset 8: Family Integration
**Core Narrative:** Taylor and Amber's role in the journey
**Emotional Core:** Why it matters, who he's building for, family resilience
**Key Details:**
- Ages during the crisis
- What they witnessed
- Conversations had (and not had)
- Their own development and growth
- The family meeting where values were established
- Teaching them what he learned

### Story Asset 9: Real Estate Wisdom
**Core Narrative:** Learning real estate the hard way and the right way
**Emotional Core:** Patience, analysis, leverage understanding
**Key Details:**
- Properties owned before 2008
- What happened to them in the crisis
- Properties acquired after - different approach
- Cashflow vs. appreciation lesson learned hard
- Current portfolio philosophy

### Story Asset 10: Building the Empire Team
**Core Narrative:** Learning to lead and delegate after being solo
**Emotional Core:** Trust, multiplication, leadership growth
**Key Details:**
- First key hire at Global Integrity
- Learning to let go of control
- Delegation failures and breakthroughs
- Building authority levels after chaos
- Creating culture that survives crisis

## Story Details NOT to Include in Early Sections

To preserve Section 9's impact, DO NOT reveal early:
- Full financial devastation numbers
- Lowest emotional moment details
- Specific suicidal ideation if applicable
- Family conflict at worst points
- Details of what was lost
- Timeline of recovery

Tease these elements with phrases like:
- "What happened next nearly broke him..."
- "The crisis that would test everything..."
- "In Section 9, you'll see how this foundation held..."
