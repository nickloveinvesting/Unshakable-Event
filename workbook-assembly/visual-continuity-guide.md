# Visual Continuity Guide
## The Unshakable Empire Workbook - Design Consistency Standards

This document establishes visual elements that create a cohesive design language across all 9 sections, ensuring the workbook feels like ONE unified transformation journey.

---

## Design Philosophy

### Core Visual Principles

1. **Consistency Creates Trust** - Same visual patterns across sections build subconscious reliability
2. **Progressive Revelation** - Visual complexity increases as reader advances
3. **Emotional Pacing** - Colors and imagery support emotional journey
4. **Action Orientation** - Design elements guide reader to action, not just reading
5. **Toby's Journey Visual Thread** - His story has consistent visual treatment throughout

## Typography System

### Font Hierarchy

| Level | Font | Weight | Size | Usage |
|-------|------|--------|------|-------|
| **Section Title** | Playfair Display | Bold | 48pt | Section opening pages only |
| **Chapter Title** | Playfair Display | SemiBold | 32pt | Chapter beginnings |
| **H1** | Montserrat | Bold | 24pt | Major section headers |
| **H2** | Montserrat | SemiBold | 20pt | Subsection headers |
| **H3** | Montserrat | Medium | 16pt | Minor headers, callout titles |
| **Body** | Open Sans | Regular | 11pt | Main content |
| **Body Bold** | Open Sans | SemiBold | 11pt | Emphasis within body |
| **Quote** | Playfair Display | Italic | 14pt | Pull quotes, Toby quotes |
| **Exercise Prompt** | Montserrat | Medium | 12pt | Exercise instructions |
| **Worksheet Fill** | Open Sans | Light | 11pt | Lines for reader writing |

### Typography Rules

1. **Never** use more than 2 fonts on a page (Playfair Display + Open Sans or Montserrat + Open Sans)
2. **Always** 1.5 line spacing for body text
3. **Minimum** 0.5" margins on all pages
4. **Left-align** body text (never full justify in exercises)
5. **Center** only major titles and single-line quotes

## Visual Progression by Section

The visual treatment subtly evolves to match the journey:

| Section | Visual Intensity | White Space | Complexity |
|---------|------------------|-------------|------------|
| 1 | Calm, spacious | High | Low |
| 2 | Energetic, active | Medium-High | Low-Medium |
| 3 | Balanced, structured | Medium | Medium |
| 4 | Confident, engaging | Medium | Medium |
| 5 | Professional, systematic | Medium | Medium-High |
| 6 | Substantial, grounded | Medium | Medium-High |
| 7 | Authoritative, expansive | Medium-Low | High |
| 8 | Warm, meaningful | Medium | Medium |
| 9 | Intense, culminating | Low | Highest |

### Section 9 Special Treatment
- Increased use of Crisis Red
- More dense layouts (crisis requires information)
- Checklists and protocols prominent
- Final "Unshakable Declaration" page is gold-bordered, certificate-style

## Image Guidelines

### Photography Style
- **Authentic over polished** - Real moments, not staged perfection
- **Diverse representation** - Multiple backgrounds, family structures
- **Action-oriented** - People doing, not just posing
- **Aspirational but attainable** - Success that feels possible

### Illustration Style
- **Clean line art** for frameworks and diagrams
- **Consistent stroke weight** across all illustrations
- **Section accent colors** for fill elements
- **Minimalist approach** - simplicity over complexity

### Toby's Visual Representation
When representing Toby:
- **Farm imagery** - Open fields, sunrise, hard work
- **Office/business imagery** - But not corporate sterile
- **Family imagery** - With Taylor and Amber (age-appropriate)
- **Recovery imagery** - Rebuilding, growth, strength
- **Never:** Ostentation, luxury for luxury's sake
