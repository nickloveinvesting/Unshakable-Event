## Key Research Findings Summary

### 1. Cashflow Quadrant & Wealth Thinking (cashflow-quadrant-wealth-thinking.md)
- Kiyosaki's 4 quadrants: Employee, Self-Employed, Business Owner, Investor
- Left side (E/S) trades time for money; Right side (B/I) builds systems and assets
- Quadrant migration requires psychological transformation, not just income change
- Financial literacy (assets vs. liabilities, income types) is the missing education
- Key researchers: Kiyosaki, Stanley & Danko, DeMarco

### 2. Profit First System (profit-first-system-implementation.md)
- Formula flip: Sales - Profit = Expenses (not Sales - Expenses = Profit)
- 5 account system: Income, Profit, Owner's Comp, Tax, OPEX
- Target Allocation Percentages (TAPs) by revenue level
- Small plates theory: Less visible money forces efficiency
- Key researcher: Mike Michalowicz

### 3. Multiple Income Streams (multiple-income-streams-strategy.md)
- 7 income streams of millionaires: Earned, Profit, Interest, Dividend, Rental, Capital Gains, Royalty
- Sequential focus: Build one stream before adding another
- Active to passive income is the wealth-building journey
- Risk mitigation through income diversification
- Key researchers: Stanley & Danko, Sabatier, Flynn

### 4. Debt Elimination Frameworks (debt-elimination-frameworks.md)
- Debt Snowball (Ramsey): Smallest balance first; psychologically superior
- Debt Avalanche: Highest interest first; mathematically optimal
- Good debt (assets that produce income) vs. Bad debt (consumption/depreciation)
- Debt-to-Income ratio determines wealth-building capacity
- Key researchers: Ramsey, Orman, Bach, Kiyosaki

### 5. Wealth Psychology & Money Mindset (wealth-psychology-money-mindset.md)
- 4 Money Scripts: Money Avoidance, Money Worship, Money Status, Money Vigilance
- Only Money Vigilance positively correlated with net worth
- Scarcity mindset impairs brain function for financial decisions
- Financial trauma is real and treatable
- Money scripts can be identified, traced, and rewritten
- Key researchers: Brad Klontz, Ted Klontz, Carol Dweck, T. Harv Eker
