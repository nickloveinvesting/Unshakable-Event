### 2. Cashflow Quadrant Diagram

**Type:** Framework Diagram (4-quadrant)
**Placement:** Part 1 - The Cashflow Quadrant
**Description:**
- Four equal quadrants labeled E, S, B, I
- E (Employee): "I have a job"
- S (Self-Employed): "I own a job"
- B (Business Owner): "I own a system"
- I (Investor): "Money works for me"
- Left side (E/S) darker shade = active income
- Right side (B/I) lighter shade = passive/leveraged income
- Arrows showing progression path E → S → B → I

**Design Elements:**
- Grid lines separating quadrants
- Clear labels in each quadrant
- "Active Income" bracket on left
- "Passive/Leveraged" bracket on right
- Progress arrows showing migration path

**Size:** Half-page or full-page spread
**File Reference:** `visual-03-01-cashflow-quadrant.png`

### 4. Profit First 5-Account Flow Diagram

**Type:** Flow Chart
**Placement:** Part 2 - Profit First System (after formula comparison)
**Description:**
```
[REVENUE RECEIVED]
        ↓
[INCOME ACCOUNT] (temporary holding)
        ↓
[ALLOCATION DAY: 10th & 25th]
        ↓
    ┌───┴───┬───────┬──────┐
    ↓       ↓       ↓      ↓
[PROFIT] [OWNER'S] [TAX] [OPEX]
 5-15%    PAY     15%   30-50%
         40-50%
```

**Design Elements:**
- Central flow from top to bottom
- Four colored containers for destination accounts
- Percentage ranges shown under each account
- Calendar icon near "10th & 25th"
- PROFIT account highlighted (this is the key insight)

**Color Coding:**
- INCOME: Gray (neutral holding)
- PROFIT: Gold/Green (priority - pay yourself first)
- OWNER'S PAY: Blue (steady salary)
- TAX: Orange (set aside, don't touch)
- OPEX: Gray (what's left for operations)

**Size:** Half-page
**File Reference:** `visual-03-03-profit-first-flow.png`

### 6. Income Stream Pyramid

**Type:** Pyramid/Hierarchy Diagram
**Placement:** Part 3 - Multiple Income Streams
**Description:**
Pyramid with 7 levels (bottom to top):
1. Earned Income (base - most people start here)
2. Profit Income (business ownership)
3. Interest Income (savings/bonds)
4. Dividend Income (stocks)
5. Rental Income (real estate)
6. Capital Gains (appreciation)
7. Royalty Income (IP - top of pyramid)

**Design Elements:**
- Pyramid structure (wide at bottom, narrow at top)
- Left side labeled "ACTIVE" at bottom, "PASSIVE" at top
- Right side shows "Phase 1," "Phase 2," etc.
- Color gradient from dark (bottom) to light (top)
- Note: "Average millionaire has 7 streams / Average person has 1"

**Size:** Half-page
**File Reference:** `visual-03-05-income-pyramid.png`

## Design Notes for Visual Team

1. **Consistency with Sections 1-2:** Maintain same visual language, fonts, and styling established in previous sections

2. **Color Coding:**
   - Section accent: Prosperity Green (#276749)
   - Positive/Good: Green tones
   - Negative/Bad: Red/orange tones
   - Neutral/Process: Gray tones

3. **Callout Box Styling:**
   - "Tough Love" = Dark/red accent
   - "Toby's Story" = Blue accent
   - "Connection/Forward Reference" = Light gray/neutral

4. **Worksheets:**
   - Fillable form fields
   - Adequate writing space
   - Clear section breaks
   - Signature/date lines where appropriate

5. **Financial Diagrams:**
   - Clean, professional look
   - Not overly complex
   - Clear labels and legends
   - Accessible for non-financial audience
