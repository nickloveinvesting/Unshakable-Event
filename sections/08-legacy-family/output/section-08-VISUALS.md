# Section 8: Visual Placement Guide
## The Unshakable Empire Workbook - Legacy and Family

---

## Section Accent Colors

**Primary Color:** Warm Gold (#D4AF37) + Amber (#FFBF00)
- Represents legacy, warmth, family, generational continuation
- Evokes precious metals (value that lasts)
- Warm and inviting (family comfort)

**Supporting Colors:**
- Deep Emerald Green (#2D5016) - growth, generational forward movement
- Soft White/Cream - purity, clarity, foundation
- Deep Charcoal Gray - stability, strength

## Typography Guidelines

**Headers:**
- Section headers: 36pt, serif, gold color
- Part headers: 24pt, serif, dark charcoal
- Subsection headers: 18pt, sans-serif, dark charcoal

**Body:**
- Regular text: 12pt, serif, dark charcoal
- Callout text: 11pt, sans-serif, dark charcoal
- Quotes/stories: 13pt, serif, italicized

**Emphasis:**
- Key concepts: Bold
- Cross-references: Reference format with section number
- Important warnings: ALL CAPS or red text (sparingly)

---

## Page Break Guidelines

- **After section overview** (before introduction)
- **After each major part** (5 parts = 5 breaks)
- **Before Exercise sections** (clear separation)
- **Before Final Summary** (new page for emphasis)
- **Before Transition to Section 9** (clean handoff)

**Estimated pages:** 25-30 pages in final layout

---

## Print Specifications

- **Paper quality:** High-quality white (not glossy)
- **Color:** Full 4-color process
- **Binding:** Perfect-bound (like a book)
- **Recommended font for printing:** Georgia (serif), Calibri (sans-serif)
- **Gold accents:** Consider metallic ink for visual impact (adds cost)
- **Images:** 300 DPI minimum for professional quality
