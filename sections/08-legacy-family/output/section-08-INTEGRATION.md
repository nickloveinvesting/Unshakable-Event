## Language Consistency Notes

### Key Terms Used Consistently:

1. **Legacy** (Not "inheritance" unless specifically legal)
   - Used 40+ times throughout
   - Emphasizes what endures, not what transfers
   - "Legacy is what remains when you're gone"

2. **Generational Wealth** (Not "inheritance" or "wealth transfer")
   - Emphasizes multi-generational scope
   - Implies wisdom + wealth together
   - "Generational wealth transfer" = process, "generational wealth" = outcome

3. **Family Mission** (Not "family plan" or "family goals")
   - More intentional than plan
   - More purpose-driven than goals
   - Creates shared identity

4. **Values Transmission** (Not "teaching values")
   - Emphasizes that what you live is what transmits
   - Implies action, not just words
   - "Values transmitted through modeling"

5. **Stewardship** (When discussing resources + faith)
   - Emphasizes responsibility, not ownership
   - Faith-aligned language
   - "We steward resources for purposes bigger than ourselves"
