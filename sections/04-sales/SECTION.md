## Key Research Findings Summary

### 1. Sales Psychology & Influence (sales-psychology-influence-principles.md)
- Cialdini's 7 principles: Reciprocity, Commitment, Social Proof, Authority, Liking, Scarcity, + Unity (2016)
- 95% of purchase decisions are made by subconscious limbic system (Zaltman, Harvard)
- Kahneman's System 1/System 2: Emotional brain decides, logical brain justifies
- Pre-suasion: What happens before the message matters as much as the message
- Key researchers: Robert Cialdini, Daniel Kahneman, Dan Ariely, Gerald Zaltman

### 2. Objection Handling (objection-handling-frameworks.md)
- 60% of customers say "no" four times before saying "yes"
- All objections fit 5 categories: Budget, Authority, Need, Timing, Trust (BANT+T)
- Salespeople who effectively handle objections are 2.4x more likely to meet quota
- Feel-Felt-Found (Zig Ziglar), LIER Method, Four P's, Question-Back frameworks
- Key insight: Objections are buying signals, not roadblocks

### 3. Relationship-Based Selling (relationship-based-selling-methodologies.md)
- Sandler: Qualification-focused, 7-step system, Pain Funnel
- Challenger: Teach-Tailor-Take Control (39% of top performers are Challengers)
- SPIN: Situation-Problem-Implication-Need-Payoff questions (35,000+ calls research)
- Consultative selling delivers 17% higher sales than transactional
- Key researchers: David Sandler, Matthew Dixon/Brent Adamson, Neil Rackham

### 4. Negotiation & Closing (negotiation-closing-techniques.md)
- Chris Voss FBI tactics: Tactical empathy, mirroring, labeling, calibrated questions
- Harvard Principled Negotiation: Separate people from problem, focus on interests
- Trial closes and assumptive closes increase conversion when used ethically
- High-pressure tactics damage trust and kill deals (Harvard/Stanford research)
- Key researchers: Chris Voss, Roger Fisher, William Ury

### 5. Pipeline Management (sales-tracking-pipeline-management.md)
- Companies with structured pipeline management see 28% higher revenue growth
- Teams tracking 5-7 KPIs achieve 91% quota attainment vs. 73% for 0-3 KPIs
- 70% of B2B reps missed quota in 2024 (Xactly Insights)
- 80% of sales require 5+ follow-ups; 44% of reps give up after one
- CRM recommendations: HubSpot (scaling), Go High Level (agencies), Pipedrive (SMB)
