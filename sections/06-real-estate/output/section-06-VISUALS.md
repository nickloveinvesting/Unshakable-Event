### Visual 6.6: Exit Strategy Decision Tree
- **Location:** Part 5 (Risk Mitigation), page ~34
- **Type:** Flowchart decision tree
- **Description:** Visual guide for choosing exit strategy based on market conditions and deal characteristics
- **Starting Question:** "What's your primary goal?"
- **Branches:**
  - Quick profit → Market hot? → Yes: Flip → No: Wholesale
  - Long-term wealth → Cashflow positive? → Yes: Hold → No: Flip or Pass

- **Each exit shows:** Timeline, typical profit, risk level
- **Colors:** Green for go paths, amber for caution, red for high-risk
- **Size:** Full page (landscape orientation recommended)

### Visual 6.7: Risk Assessment Matrix
- **Location:** Part 5 (Risk Mitigation), page ~36
- **Type:** Grid/matrix
- **Description:** 5x5 matrix showing risk factors by likelihood and impact
- **Axes:**
  - X-axis: Likelihood (Low to High)
  - Y-axis: Impact (Low to High)
- **Plotted risks:**
  - High Impact/High Likelihood: ARV overestimate, Rehab underestimate
  - High Impact/Low Likelihood: Market crash, Title issues
  - Low Impact/High Likelihood: Minor repairs, Closing delays
- **Color zones:** Green (acceptable), Yellow (caution), Red (avoid)
- **Size:** Half page

## Worksheet Designs

### Worksheet 6.1: Deal Analysis Template
- **Format:** 2-page spread
- **Page 1 Content:**
  - Property Information (address, asking price, beds/baths, sq ft)
  - Comparable Sales Grid (5 rows for comps with columns: Address, Sale Price, Sq Ft, Bed/Bath, $/SF, Adjustments, Adjusted Value)
  - ARV Calculation Box (with conservative methodology)
- **Page 2 Content:**
  - Rehab Estimate Summary (major categories with cost estimates)
  - MAO Calculation (step-by-step formula boxes)
  - Deal Decision Box (Go/No-Go with profit projection)
- **Design:** Calculator-style with fill-in boxes, forest green accents
- **Special Features:** Built-in formula reminders, margin notes for common mistakes

### Worksheet 6.2: Scope of Work Template
- **Format:** 4-page checklist document
- **Sections:**
  - **Exterior:** Roof, Siding, Windows, Doors, Landscaping, Driveway, Foundation visible
  - **Kitchen:** Cabinets, Countertops, Appliances, Flooring, Backsplash, Lighting, Plumbing
  - **Bathrooms:** Vanity, Toilet, Tub/Shower, Tile, Flooring, Fixtures (per bathroom)
  - **Living Areas:** Paint, Flooring, Trim, Doors, Lighting, Outlets
  - **Systems:** HVAC, Electrical Panel, Plumbing, Water Heater, Insulation
- **Columns for each item:**
  - Current Condition (1-5 scale)
  - Repair/Replace/Skip
  - Description of Work Needed
  - Estimated Cost (Low/High range)
- **Bottom of each page:** Running subtotal
- **Final page:** Grand total + Contingency calculation (checkbox for 15%/20%/25%/30%)

### Worksheet 6.3: Financing Decision Tree
- **Format:** Full page flowchart + reference table
- **Flowchart Questions:**
  1. Deal type? (Flip / Rental / Wholesale / BRRRR)
  2. Timeline needed? (7-14 days / 30 days / 45+ days)
  3. Credit score? (720+ / 680-719 / Below 680)
  4. Capital available? (25%+ / 10-24% / Under 10%)
  5. Existing relationships? (Private lenders / Hard money only / Bank only)
- **Each path leads to:** Recommended financing with cost estimate
- **Reference Table:** Quick comparison of all options with cost per $100k borrowed

### Worksheet 6.4: Market Research Template
- **Format:** Full page analysis grid (landscape)
- **Structure:** 3 columns for comparing 3 markets side-by-side
- **Row Categories:**
  - **Demographics:** Population, Growth %, Median Income, Unemployment
  - **Real Estate:** Median Price, Price Growth, Rent/Price Ratio, Days on Market
  - **Economic:** Major Employers, Job Growth, New Construction Permits
  - **Investment:** Investor Activity, Wholesale Volume, Flip Success Rate
- **Scoring Section:** 1-10 rating for each factor, total score per market
- **Decision Box:** Selected market with rationale

### Worksheet 6.5: Risk Assessment Checklist
- **Format:** Full page checklist with mitigation planning
- **Risk Categories:**
  - ARV Risk (3 items to check)
  - Rehab Risk (4 items to check)
  - Market Risk (3 items to check)
  - Financing Risk (3 items to check)
  - Exit Risk (3 items to check)
- **For Each Risk:**
  - Checkbox for identified
  - Severity rating (1-5)
  - Mitigation strategy (write-in)
  - Backup plan (write-in)
- **Bottom Section:**
  - Primary Exit Strategy
  - Backup Exit Strategy (Plan B)
  - Final Decision: Proceed / Pass / Need More Information

## Page Layout Guidelines

### Standard Content Pages
- **Header:** Section number + title in forest green bar
- **Body:** Two-column layout for dense content, single column for exercises
- **Margin notes:** Quick tips and cross-references in green italic
- **Footer:** Page number + "Unshakable Empire Workbook"

### Exercise Pages
- **Full-width layout**
- **Exercise header bar:** Forest green with white text
- **Instruction boxes:** Numbered steps with adequate white space
- **Worksheet integration:** Reference to worksheet number and page
- **Time estimate:** Clock icon with duration

### Transition Pages
- **Section recap in summary box**
- **Forward reference in teaser box**
- **Section icon repeated at reduced size
- **"Section X Complete" footer

## Typography Specifications

### Headers
- **H1 (Section Title):** 28pt, Bold, Forest Green, ALL CAPS
- **H2 (Part Titles):** 24pt, Bold, Navy, Title Case
- **H3 (Major Subsections):** 18pt, Bold, Forest Green, Title Case
- **H4 (Minor Subsections):** 14pt, Bold, Black, Sentence case

### Body Text
- **Standard:** 11pt, Regular, Black
- **Emphasis:** 11pt, Bold, Black
- **Quotes:** 11pt, Italic, Navy
- **Callouts:** 10pt, Regular, varies by box type

### Special Elements
- **Formulas:** 12pt, Monospace, in shaded box
- **Tables:** 10pt, with alternating row colors
- **Worksheet fields:** 10pt, with underline or box for entry
