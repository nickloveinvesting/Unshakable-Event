- "Cashflow" (one word, not "cash flow")
- "Fix-and-flip" (hyphenated)
- "Buy-and-hold" (hyphenated)
- "Conservative analysis" (not "careful" or "cautious")
- "Deal analysis" (not "property evaluation")
- "Real estate" (two words, not "realestate" or "RE" in body text)

### Voice Consistency:
- Direct, empowering second person ("You will learn...")
- Toby quotes in first person ("I coached a student who...")
- Never preachy ("You should...") — always empowering ("You choose...")
