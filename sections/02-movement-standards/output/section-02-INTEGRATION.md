- Declaration ("I am becoming...")
- Awareness (beliefs, archetypes, narrative)
- Commitment (signed declaration)

## Key Decisions Made

### Structure Decisions

1. **Opened with story, then science** - Per feedback, 4:30 AM farm story comes first (2-3 pages), THEN habit science explained
2. **Tough Love callouts distributed** - 4 callouts spread across section (intro, Part 1, Part 3, Part 4)
3. **5 exercises (vs 8 in exercise progression doc)** - Consolidated for focus and completion likelihood
4. **28-Day Challenge as centerpiece** - Made this the signature exercise, not just one of many

### Content Decisions

1. **Simplified academic language** - Led with plain English, added technical terms in parentheses
2. **Directive tone throughout** - Commands, imperatives, "do this now" language
3. **Multiple Toby stories** - Three distinct stories plus quotes (farm, Imperial building, post-2008)
4. **Forward references as callout boxes** - Made explicit connections to future sections visible

### Integration Decisions

1. **Section 1 elements become tools** - Identity Declaration = morning affirmation; Pain vs. Vision = fuel
2. **Milestone page added** - Foundation Complete milestone after Section 2 (per visual continuity guide)
3. **Crisis anchor planted early** - Multiple references to Section 9 as ultimate test of disciplines
