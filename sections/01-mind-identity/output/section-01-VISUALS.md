# Section 1: Visual Placement Guide
## The Unshakable Empire Workbook - Mind & Identity

---

## Section Overview

This document specifies all visual elements required for Section 1, including diagrams, icons, worksheets, and callout box styling. All visuals follow the standards established in `workbook-assembly/visual-continuity-guide.md`.

**Section Accent Color:** Deep Purple (#553C9A)
**Primary Color:** Empire Navy (#1A365D)
**Highlight Color:** Unshakable Gold (#D4AF37)

---

## Visual Elements by Location

### Visual 1.1: Section Icon - Mind & Identity

**Location:** Section title page (full page opener)
**Type:** Icon
**Description:** Stylized brain with upward growth arrow emerging from crown, symbolizing identity transformation and mental development
**Design Specifications:**
- Brain outline in Empire Navy (#1A365D)
- Growth arrow in Deep Purple (#553C9A)

### Visual 1.2: Brain Regions Diagram - Neuroscience Section

**Location:** Part 1, after "Key Brain Regions in Identity" table
**Type:** Diagram
**Description:** Simplified brain cross-section showing three key regions involved in identity
**Design Specifications:**

---

### Visual 1.4: Archetype Wheel

**Location:** Part 3, after the archetype tables
**Type:** Diagram
**Description:** Circular wheel showing all 12 archetypes arranged by development stage
**Design Specifications:**
- Four quadrants representing stages:
  - Preparation (top-left): Innocent, Orphan, Warrior, Caregiver
  - Journey (top-right): Seeker, Destroyer, Lover, Creator
  - Return (bottom-right): Ruler, Magician
  - Transformation (bottom-left): Sage, Jester
- Each archetype in its own segment
- Quadrant labels in small text
- Center text: "Your Archetypal Journey"
- Color scheme: Gradient of Deep Purple to Empire Navy
- Clean, modern design (not medieval/mythological)
**Size:** Half-page (approximately 5" x 5")
**File needed:** `section-01-archetype-wheel.svg`

---

### Visual 1.5: Goals vs. Identity Comparison Chart

**Location:** Part 5, after "Why Goals Fail (But Identity Succeeds)"
**Type:** Comparison Chart
**Description:** Two-column comparison showing the difference between goal-based and identity-based approaches
**Design Specifications:**
- Left column header: "GOAL-BASED" (gray background)
- Right column header: "IDENTITY-BASED" (Deep Purple background)
- Comparison rows:
  - Focus: Outcomes vs. Who you are
  - Timeframe: Temporary vs. Permanent
  - Motivation: External vs. Internal
  - Sustainability: Low vs. High
  - Example: "I want $1M" vs. "I am a wealth builder"
- Checkmark icons on identity side in Growth Green
- X marks on goal side in Crisis Red
**Size:** Half-page width
**File needed:** `section-01-goals-vs-identity.svg`

## Worksheet Specifications

### Worksheet 1.1: Thought Record & Belief Audit

**Location:** Exercise 1.1
**Type:** Fill-in worksheet
**Format:** Full page, multi-section layout
**Design Specifications:**

**Section A - Core Belief Audit:**
- 8 rows with sentence stems
- Wide fill-in lines after each stem
- Header: "CORE BELIEF AUDIT" in Montserrat Bold

**Section B - 7-Day Thought Record:**
- Table with 8 columns
- Columns: Situation | Automatic Thought | Emotion (0-100) | Distortion | Evidence For | Evidence Against | Balanced Thought | New Emotion (0-100)
- 5-7 empty rows for entries
- Header: "THOUGHT RECORD" in Montserrat Bold

**Styling:**
- Section icon (brain) in header
- Writing action icon in top right
- Deep Purple accent on left border
- Clean table lines in Foundation Stone
- Sufficient space for handwriting (11pt minimum line height)

**Pages:** 2 pages (front and back)
**File needed:** `worksheet-01-01-thought-record.pdf`

---

### Worksheet 1.2: Archetype Assessment

**Location:** Exercise 1.2
**Type:** Assessment worksheet
**Format:** 2-page spread
**Design Specifications:**

**Page 1 - Self-Assessment:**
- Table with 3 columns: Archetype | Resonance (1-10) | Evidence from Your Life
- 12 rows for each archetype
- Rating scale reminder at top

**Page 2 - Shadow Work:**
- Two sections with 5 numbered lines each:
  - "Qualities in others that trigger negative reactions"
  - "Qualities you admire but feel you lack"
- Integration section at bottom with fill-in fields:
  - My primary archetype is: _______
  - My secondary archetype is: _______
  - My shadow archetype is: _______
  - This explains my past behavior when: _______

**Styling:**
- Archetype wheel mini-reference in corner
- Deep Purple accent elements
- Generous writing space

**Pages:** 2 pages
**File needed:** `worksheet-01-02-archetype.pdf`

---

### Worksheet 1.3: Life Timeline & Story Reframe

**Location:** Exercise 1.3
**Type:** Visual + narrative worksheet
**Format:** 2-page spread
**Design Specifications:**

**Page 1 - Timeline:**
- Horizontal timeline spanning page width
- "Birth" on left, "Present" on right
- Chapter division markers (childhood, adolescence, young adult, etc.)
- Space above and below line for marking HIGH (up arrows) and LOW (down arrows) points
- Legend: ⬆ = High Point, ⬇ = Low Point

**Page 2 - Narrative Work:**
- Section: "Narrative Analysis" with 4 question prompts
- Section: "Redemptive Reframe" with two large text boxes:
  - "Contamination Version:" (gray border)
  - "Redemptive Version:" (Deep Purple border)
- Section: "The Arc of Your Story" with 3 reflection prompts

**Styling:**
- Timeline in Empire Navy
- Writing spaces in Warm Cream (#FFFAF0) background
- Book icon (narrative symbol) in header

**Pages:** 2 pages
**File needed:** `worksheet-01-03-life-timeline.pdf`

---

### Worksheet 1.4: Identity Declaration

**Location:** Exercise 1.4
**Type:** Declaration worksheet
**Format:** Full page, certificate-style
**Design Specifications:**

**Top Section - Foundation Work:**
- 4 fill-in fields drawing from previous exercises
- Smaller text, functional design

**Middle Section - Declaration Building:**
- "I am becoming a person who..." sentence starter
- Behavior evidence: 5 numbered lines
- Outcome evidence: 5 numbered lines

**Bottom Section - Full Declaration:**
- Large text box (5+ lines) with decorative border
- Unshakable Gold (#D4AF37) thin border
- Header: "MY IDENTITY DECLARATION" in Playfair Display Bold

**Signature Area:**
- Signature line with "Signature" label
- Date line with "Date" label
- Statement: "I commit to reading this declaration aloud daily for 30 days"

**Styling:**
- Certificate-style elegance
- Deep Purple accent in corners
- Formal but not stuffy

**Pages:** 1 page
**File needed:** `worksheet-01-04-declaration.pdf`

---

### Worksheet 1.5: Pain vs. Vision Ledger

**Location:** Exercise 1.5
**Type:** Two-column ledger worksheet
**Format:** Full page, ledger layout
**Design Specifications:**

**Layout:**
- Two equal columns side by side
- Left column header: "PAIN - What I'm Running FROM" (Crisis Red accent)
- Right column header: "VISION - What I'm Running TOWARD" (Growth Green accent)
- 10 numbered lines in each column
- Circle instruction for "most powerful" item in each column

**Bottom Section:**
- Integration statement with fill-in structure:
  - "I am becoming [vision identity] _______
  - so I never return to [pain identity] _______."

**Styling:**
- Contrasting column headers (red vs. green)
- Clean dividing line between columns
- Target icon in header

**Pages:** 1 page
**File needed:** `worksheet-01-05-pain-vision.pdf`

## Callout Box Specifications

### Callout Type 1: Toby's Tough Love

**Instances:** 1 (Introduction section)
**Design:**
- Background: Light red/salmon tint
- Border: 3px solid Crisis Red (#C53030) on left
- Header: "TOBY'S TOUGH LOVE" in Montserrat Bold, red
- Icon: Warning triangle or directness symbol
- Text: Open Sans, slightly condensed line spacing
- Corner treatment: Slightly rounded (4px radius)

---

### Callout Type 2: Toby's Story

**Instances:** 4 (Part 1, Part 3, Part 4/Teaser, Part 5)
**Design:**
- Background: Warm Cream (#FFFAF0)
- Border: 2px solid Unshakable Gold (#D4AF37), full box
- Header: "TOBY'S JOURNEY" with book icon
- Corner treatment: Rounded (8px radius)
- Text: Open Sans with Playfair Display for quoted portions

---

### Callout Type 3: Cross-Reference / Connection Boxes

**Instances:** 5 (After Ex 1.1, after Ex 1.2, after Ex 1.3, Part 5 forward refs, after Ex 1.5)
**Design:**
- Background: Light gray (#F7FAFC)
- Border: 2px dashed, color matching referenced section
- Header: "CONNECTION" with link icon
- Text referencing specific section/exercise numbers
- Arrow pointing to referenced section icon

---

### Callout Type 4: Toby's Final Word

**Instances:** 1 (Summary section)
**Design:**
- Background: Light Deep Purple tint
- Border: 3px solid Deep Purple (#553C9A)
- Header: "TOBY'S FINAL WORD" in Montserrat Bold
- Larger text size than other callouts (12pt vs 11pt)
- Full-width box (not sidebar)

## Image/Photography Guidelines

For Section 1, any photography should convey:
- **Introspection and clarity** - People in thoughtful moments
- **Transformation potential** - Before/after or journey imagery
- **Agricultural roots** - Subtle farm imagery for Toby story sections
- **Brain/mind representations** - Abstract neural patterns if needed

Avoid:
- Overly clinical/medical brain imagery
- Corporate stock photos
- Posed motivational speaker shots

## Production Checklist

Before finalizing Section 1 visuals, verify:

### Color
- [ ] Deep Purple (#553C9A) used as section accent
- [ ] Empire Navy (#1A365D) for primary headers
- [ ] Unshakable Gold (#D4AF37) for achievements/key moments only
- [ ] No unauthorized colors introduced

### Icons
- [ ] Brain/growth icon appears in page headers throughout section
- [ ] Writing icon on all exercise headers
- [ ] Link icon on all cross-reference callouts
- [ ] Book icon on all Toby Story callouts

### Callout Boxes
- [ ] Max 3 callout boxes per 2-page spread
- [ ] Toby Story boxes not adjacent to each other
- [ ] Cross-reference boxes included where noted
- [ ] Correct box type used for each instance

### Worksheets
- [ ] All 5 worksheets designed and tested for print
- [ ] Sufficient writing space for handwriting
- [ ] Headers and instructions clear
- [ ] Page numbers included

### Progress Elements
- [ ] Progress tracker on section opener
- [ ] Completion checklist before transition
- [ ] Transition arrow to Section 2
