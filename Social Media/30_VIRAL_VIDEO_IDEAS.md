# 30 Viral Video Ideas for IGNITE Program

**Created:** November 25, 2025
**Purpose:** Drive high demand for IGNITE program through viral, emotionally-resonant content
**Target:** 500 members on Monday morning calls within 45 days

---

## Executive Summary

These 30 video ideas are strategically designed based on analysis of top business influencers (Alex Hormozi, Andy Elliott, Tony Robbins, Brendon Burchard, Russell Brunson) and the specific needs of IGNITE's target audience. Each video addresses a core pain point while naturally leading viewers toward the IGNITE program.

**Key Insights Applied:**
- **Entertainment + Emotion = Viral** (not just education)
- **Raw, authentic content outperforms polished** (meeting feedback: landlord drama got 60-130K views)
- **Story-based vulnerability** builds trust faster than success flexing
- **Polarizing opinions** generate engagement through debate
- **Specific numbers and timeframes** create credibility and shareability

### Video #1: "You're Not Stuck. You're Comfortable."

**Hook (First 3 seconds):** "Stop lying to yourself. You're not stuck—you're comfortable in your misery."

**Core Message:** Most people confuse comfort with safety. The pain of staying the same hasn't exceeded the pain of change yet.

**Format:** YouTube Short / Reel / TikTok
**Duration:** 60 seconds
**Target Pain Point:** Feeling stuck but not taking action
**Viral Potential:** 9/10 - Highly shareable, confrontational, creates debate

**Content Outline:**
1. Hook: Direct accusation that reframes "stuck" as choice
2. Problem agitation: "You say you hate your job, but you won't quit. You say you want more, but you won't sacrifice."
3. Story/Example: "I've coached people making $50K complaining they're stuck while turning down opportunities. You know what they're actually afraid of? Success."
4. Solution/Insight: "The moment you admit you're choosing comfort over growth, everything changes."
5. Soft CTA: "Be honest with yourself. What are you tolerating that you shouldn't be?"

**Repurposing Notes:**
- Long-form expansion: 10-min deep dive on "The Comfort Trap"
- 60-sec clip version: Use strongest 15 seconds as teaser
- Instagram adaptation: Text overlay with hard-hitting statements
- LinkedIn angle: "Why Your Top Performers Are Actually Your Biggest Flight Risks"

**IGNITE Connection:** IGNITE's accountability structure forces weekly confrontation with comfort zones
